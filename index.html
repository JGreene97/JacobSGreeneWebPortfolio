A BIT ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Jacob Greene, and I am a Cyber Risk Consultant at Deloitte. Currently, my role within the firm is managing and assisting a large media client in moving their on-premise infrastructure into the cloud. Previously in this position, I have helped plan, architect, build and implement new cloud managed service providers utilizing Amazon (AWS) and Google (GCP) platforms. Although I helped provide infrastructure-as-code for almost all portions of the cloud, my primary focus was to fully develop infrastructure-as-code services for the Identity and Access Management (IAM) and DevOps CI/CD pipelines.

I completed my masters degree in computer science from University of North Carolina - Charlotte in 2020, which has led to much of the opportunities I am pursuing today. I consider myself a "forever student," eager to build on my academic foundations in computer science, and stay in tune with the latest development strategies, fundamentals, and frameworks through continued professional development and personal projects.

My hunger for knowledge and determination to progress within the world of technology has contributed to much of my success. With my position at Deloitte, I have been assigned as one of our key AWS and GCP Cloud subject matter experts (SME's), and have been sent to many learning and certification programs in order to continue to grow and share my knowledge with teams across the firm.

I believe mindfulness in the workplace is key to success, which means allowing time to explore other hobbies outside of the workplace. I enjoy several different hobbies - working on cars, playing golf with friends, going to the gym, or simply spending quality time with family and friends.
