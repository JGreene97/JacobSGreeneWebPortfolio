A BIT ABOUT ME ✍

Hello, my name is Jacob Greene, and I am a Cyber Risk Consultant at Deloitte. Currently, my role within the firm is managing and assisting a large media client move their on-premise infrastructure into the cloud. Previously in this position, I helped plan, architect, build and implement new cloud managed service providers utilizing Amazon (AWS) and Google (GCP) cloud platforms. Although I helped provide infrastructure-as-code across a large portion of cloud technologies, my primary focus was to fully develop infrastructure-as-code services for the Identity and Access Management (IAM) and DevOps CI/CD pipelines.

I completed my masters degree in computer science from University of North Carolina - Charlotte in 2020. I consider myself a "forever student," eager to build on my academic foundations in technology, and stay in tune with the latest development strategies, fundamentals, and frameworks through continued professional development and personal projects.

My hunger for knowledge and determination to progress within technology has contributed to much of my success. I have been assigned as one of our key AWS and GCP Cloud subject matter experts (SME's) at Deloitte, and have been sent to many learning and certification programs in order to continue to grow and share my knowledge with teams across the firm.

I believe mindfulness in the workplace is key to success, which means allowing time to explore other hobbies outside of the workplace. Working on cars, playing golf with friends, going to the gym, or simply spending quality time with family and friends are just a few of the hobbies partake in outside of work.

As I am looking to change positions to fulfill my passion in software development and .NET programming professionally, please do not hesitate to reach out to me! You can find all of my contact info by clicking the "CONTACT ME" button below! Wish to speak to you soon!
